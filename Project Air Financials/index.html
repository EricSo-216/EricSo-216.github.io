Metric definitions
C2 = Revenue

Gross profit / total fuel revenue. Not available in source data for 2023.

C4 = EBITDA / NOI

Net operating income after all opex (incl. R&M) before depreciation and amortisation. Core income metric.

C4++ = EBIT

C4 minus total D&A (Depre + ROU). Bottom-line profit. Only Prince Edward Rd E achieved positive EBIT in 2025.

Depre = Depreciation

Asset writedown — equipment, tanks, canopy structures. Residual D&A after amortisation.

ROU = Amortisation

Right-of-use lease amortisation (IFRS 16). Dominant D&A charge (~90% of total). Represents capitalised land lease obligation.

Volume (KL)

Fuel throughput in kilolitres. Declining at −12.6% CAGR 2023–2025, driven by EV substitution.
